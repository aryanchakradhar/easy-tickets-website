Pokhara
Pokhara is the capital of Gandaki province. It is a popular tourist destination in Nepal. Pokhara is also well-known for its adventure activities such as boating, trekking, rafting, canoeing, and bungee jumping. It has a famous Phewa lake. The entire city is surrounded by green forests and mountains.
