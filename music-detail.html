One Musical Fest
One Musical Fest is the most popular musical festival in Nepal. It is organized by the leading musical community and it is celebrated for 3 days. In this musical festival, many top Nepali singers perform their original songs. This festival is a very good opportunity for fans to connect with their favorite musical artists as the festival organizes Meet and Greet sessions on the last day of the festival.
Date: 8th July 2023
Location: Kathmandu
Available tickets: 120
Price: $10
